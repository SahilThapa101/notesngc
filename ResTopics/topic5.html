Ecstacy


You have created a stir in my heart,
This I know, by the change I have experienced,
Oh! How lovely your company feels
Diving deep into the other world amidst pure ambience.

Before, my heart felt empty, dark and lonely,
Then you came holding your light, bright and shiny,
You asked, whether you should stay,
I instantly replied, in you my foundations lay.

Since then, it feels ages, time is of no relevance,
Your promises are my inspiration, ever present,
Waiting for the promised land has never been so easy
For your grace comforts me effortlessly.




Work In Progress...


A child comes to call me for the feast,
to a table full of mince, amidst gathering full of beings.
I try to look for a kind smile,
But all I find is treachery
hidden in a veil of snary lies.

Hearts of stone need His presence
For this is the way that He recommends
Deep inside, those beings have His hidden light
which has been further stifled by authoritarian heights.

Honey gets created when the beings are submissive,
Holiness as well as holistic view then overshadows their deep inner spaces.
It is better this way to hope for a better world,
For we all are work in progress supported by His merciful graces.
