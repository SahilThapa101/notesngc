GDP(Gross Domestic Product)- this term has always been the center of modern world economy. It is seen as something which tells how prosperous a nation is and what quality of life it's citizen is experiencing on a day to day basis.

But, some people have always felt something missing from the formulation of the fabric of how GDP is calculated, more precisely, the conventional formula doesn't include the "happiness coefficient" which plays an important role in determining how smoothly the major to minor business of our modern society is carried out.
